Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

TRUECARE PROPERTY SOLUTIONS LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and how we handle SMS consent.

Personal Information We Collect

We may collect name, phone number, email address, mailing or service address, company name, service request information, work order details, appointment details, property service notes, and messages sent to our company.

How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to respond to inquiries, coordinate property preservation and maintenance services, send work order assignments, appointment reminders, scheduling updates, inspection notifications, service status updates, vendor coordination messages, and payment-related operational notices.

Who Personal Information Is Shared With

We may share limited information with authorized employees, contractors, vendors, inspectors, service providers, clients, property owners, asset managers, mortgage servicing companies, technology providers, and legal or compliance parties when necessary to operate our business or fulfill service requests.

SMS Communications and Consent

TRUECARE PROPERTY SOLUTIONS LLC may use SMS/text messaging to send Customer Care communications related to property preservation and maintenance services. Message types may include service requests, appointment scheduling, inspection updates, service status notifications, customer support, access confirmations, vendor coordination, and operational property service communications.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates.

SMS opt-in data and consent will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
