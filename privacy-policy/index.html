Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

TRUECARE PROPERTY SOLUTIONS LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and how we handle SMS consent.

Personal Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Mailing address or service address
  • Company name and business contact details
  • Service request information
  • Appointment details and property service notes
  • Messages, inquiries, and communications sent to our company

How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to operate our business and provide property preservation and maintenance services. This may include:

  • Responding to service requests and business inquiries
  • Coordinating property preservation, inspection, repair, and maintenance services
  • Sending appointment reminders, scheduling updates, inspection notifications, service status updates, and customer support communications
  • Communicating with customers and service requestors regarding active service requests
  • Maintaining business records and service documentation
  • Improving our services and internal operations
  • Complying with legal, regulatory, carrier, and business requirements

SMS Communications and Consent

By submitting a contact form and selecting the SMS consent checkbox, users expressly consent to receive Customer Care text messages from TRUECARE PROPERTY SOLUTIONS LLC.

SMS messages may include service request updates, appointment scheduling, inspection updates, service status notifications, and customer support communications.

Messaging frequency may vary. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

SMS opt-in data and consent will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Who We Share Personal Information With

We may share limited personal information only when necessary to operate our business, provide services, or comply with applicable requirements. This may include sharing information with:

  • Authorized employees and representatives of TRUECARE PROPERTY SOLUTIONS LLC
  • Service providers involved in fulfilling a property service request
  • Clients, property owners, asset managers, mortgage servicing companies, or business partners related to an active service request
  • Technology providers that help us operate our website, communications, email, phone systems, and business tools
  • Legal, regulatory, or compliance parties when required by law or necessary to protect our rights

We do not sell personal information. We do not share SMS consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request updates or corrections to your information. You may opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP. You may request assistance by replying HELP or contacting us directly.
